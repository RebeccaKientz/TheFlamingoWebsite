About The Flamingo

The Flamingo Motel of Coeur d’Alene is a local and family owned property located in the heart of Northern Idaho. The Flamingo Motel is located in the in “Old Towne” area of Coeur d’Alene which has numerous restaurants/bars, galleries, gift shops, and options for nightlife, all within a short walk.

Also located nearby you will find Pend Oreille Lake, City Park and Beach, Lake Coeur d’Alene Resort, and North Idaho Centennial Trail to explore.

The Flamingo offers 9 themed rooms and 2 two-bedroom vacation rental homes. All guest rooms include a microwave, coffee maker, refrigerator, cable television, WIFI, and hair dryers. We also have a paperback “library”, our policy is, “leave yours, take ours”.

Come visit us at The Flamingo and you’ll find the comfort and amenities you desire at an affordable price.

We welcome you and know you’ll enjoy your visit to the beautiful Coeur d’Alene area.
